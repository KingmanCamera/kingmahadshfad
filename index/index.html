About Us

My name is Scott, I'm super-tall, and I have been a photographer and videographer for almost 2 decades. I'm grateful to call the Northwest Arizona desert my home. I got my start filming MMA Fights, Music, and Corporate Videos in Boise, ID. During a 12-year career in Corporate America, I acted as a sales strategist, trainer, underwriter, and executive client relations within Fortune 100 Companies like Directv, Verizon, and Citi. We relocated to Arizona on 1-1-21 and opened a successful contracting business in the spring. I started Kingman Camera, about 18 months later, after suffering an injury that ultimately ended my contracting business. I am VERY excited to offer my services to you, I look forward to building meaningful business partnerships! I study Architectural Photography techniques, and have a passion for photographing the Night Sky, Landscape, and Aerial Cinematography. You can find me on the Colorado River, hiking in a Joshua Tree Forest, or driving these desert backroads. Thanks for stopping by, I truly appreciate your time!
